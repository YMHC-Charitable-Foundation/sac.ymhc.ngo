# Translation Terminology Glossary (EN/FR/ES/ZH)

Purpose: Keep terminology consistent across future AI translations for this site (School Attendance Challenges content by Youth Mental Health Canada).

## Core Rules For Translators

1. Prefer the glossary terms below over ad-hoc alternatives.
2. Keep brand names and acronyms unchanged unless noted: `Youth Mental Health Canada (YMHC)`, `YMHC ASAP Model`, `MTSS`, `SAC`.
3. Use person-first, non-blaming language in all languages.
4. Keep meaning stable across pages (docs, survey report, story wall, resources, and UI labels).
5. If a term is not listed, choose wording consistent with this glossary and add it here.

## Controlled Style Choices

- Prefer `School Attendance Challenges` (or localized equivalent above) over blame-oriented wording.
- Avoid translating `YMHC`, `ASAP`, and `MTSS` acronyms.
- Keep `Story Wall` as a branded section name when used as a title; translate only in sentence-level descriptive text if needed.
- Keep product/workbook titles unchanged unless an official localized edition exists.
